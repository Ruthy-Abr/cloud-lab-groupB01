CloudSim Summary

[cite: 105]

This lab uses CloudSim to simulate cloud computing infrastructures including datacenters, hosts, virtual machines (VMs), and cloudlets[cite: 106]. It allows us to test and evaluate resource scheduling algorithms, energy consumption, and performance metrics in a controlled, repeatable simulation environment without incurring the costs or complexities of renting real cloud servers.
